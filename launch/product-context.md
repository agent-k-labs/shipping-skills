# Product Context — shipping-skills

**Product name:** shipping-skills

**One-line description:** Open-source AI agent skills for everything after the AI builds your app — launch, first users, growth.

**Target user:** Vibe coders and solo founders who just shipped something with Bolt, Lovable, v0, Replit, or Claude Code and don't know what to do next.

**Core pain:** "The AI built my app in a weekend, but I have 3 users and they're all friends. Every marketing guide assumes I have a team, a budget, or an audience."

**Core promise:** Your coding agent becomes your launch team — it audits your site before launch, checks real domain availability, plans your Product Hunt day, and writes your first-100-users playbook, personalized to your product.

**Key differentiator:** Not build-step skills — these pick up where the builders leave off. Skills check instead of guess (bundled scripts hit RDAP, your live site's meta tags, robots.txt), every skill ships eval test cases, and the whole repo follows the agentskills.io spec so it installs into 70+ agents.

**Current stage:** live (v1.0.0 pushed, 14 skills)

**Pricing model:** Free, MIT-licensed. (DFY sibling: SoloMax for launch videos.)

**Primary launch channels:** r/ClaudeAI, Show HN, Indie Hackers, builder Discords (Lovable/Bolt/Claude), X build-in-public, agent-skills directories.

**Built with:** Markdown + Node/Bash scripts, following the Agent Skills spec; developed with Claude Code.

**First 30-day goal:** 200 GitHub stars and 50 tracked `npx skills add` installs.

**Known objections:** "Isn't this just prompts?" (no — trigger-tuned descriptions, bundled verification scripts, evals with a measured 1.0 vs 0.61 pass-rate delta) / "Claude can already do this without skills" (the evals measure exactly that gap).

**Comparable alternatives:** coreyhaines31/marketingskills (marketing for operators; shipping-skills is the post-build journey for vibe coders), generic prompt collections, hiring a growth consultant.
